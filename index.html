How to Play

Game Rules:

  • Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) on an empty cell
  • First player is assigned X, second player is O
  • A player wins by getting 3 of their symbols in a row:
    • Horizontally (across)
    • Vertically (up and down)
    • Diagonally
  • If all cells are filled and no player has won, the game is a draw

How to Start:

  • Create a new multiplayer game and share the room ID with a friend
  • You can also join an existing game using a room ID
  • Crea te a new single player game and play against the computer
  • Wait for your turn and click any empty cell to make a move
